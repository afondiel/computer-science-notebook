# Audio Recognition Tasks



| Audio Task              | Description                                        | Typical Use Cases                    | Real-World Examples                         |
| ----------------------- | -------------------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------ | ------------------------------------------- |
| Audio Tagging           | Multi-label classification of sounds               | Environmental tagging, music tagging | Bird call detection, Spotify genre tags     |
| Sound Event Detection   | Temporal localization and classification of events | Surveillance, wildlife monitoring    | Detecting alarms, animal call timestamps    |
| Audio Classification    | General classification of audio clips              | Speaker ID, language detection       | Call center speaker classification          |
| Speaker Recognition     | Identify or verify speaker identity                | Voice authentication                 | Phone unlocking by voice                    |
| Acoustic Scene Class.   | Classify recording environment                     | Context-aware apps                   | Detecting office vs. street environment     |
| Music Information Ret.  | Musical feature extraction                         | Music recommendation, transcription  | Shazam song ID, karaoke apps                |
| Speech Recognition      | Transcribe spoken language                         | Assistants, subtitling               | Google Assistant commands, YouTube captions |
| Emotion Recognition     | Detect emotional state in speech                   | Customer service evaluation          | Stress detection in calls                   |
| Audio Source Separation | Separate individual sounds                         | Remixing, noise cancellation         | Separating vocals/instruments in music      |
| Audio Enhancement       | Remove noise, improve audio quality                | Teleconferencing, hearing aids       | Noise suppression in Zoom calls             |
